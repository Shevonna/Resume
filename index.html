Work History

Throughout the beginning of my working career I have held positions in sales and customer service. These positions heightened my communication and organizational skills, which heightened my efficiency and appreciation from colleagues. My most recent positions have molded me into the well-rounded person I am today.

In 2016 I became an Engineering Estimator for Pacific Gas and Electric. My duties were conduct meetings with customers and their contractors, participate in in house construction meetings, develop site plans for construction, submit permit request, and be the point person to all parties involved. This position taught me how to keep a strict schedule, communicate efficiently, and foresee/anticipate future needs. I developed an understanding of what it is to be a project manager.

Although I enjoyed my time at PG&E, it was time for me to move on to a permanent position. I was hired on as an Auto Damage Estimator for Geico January 2018. This position melded together my position at PG&E with my experience as a Sales Consultant wit Carmax. I already understood what it took to be an effective project manager, and had a basic understanding of cars, making this new position tailor made for me.

The key trait that I developed during my time with Geico was Negotiation. When going into a body shop, you have to negotiate labor times and rates. Being able to justify your reasoning and walking the shop personnel through the logic behind your proposal developed my critical thinking and communication skills immensely.
